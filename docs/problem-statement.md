# Problem Statement

College students face significant challenges in preparing for campus placements:

1. **Lack of Personalized Guidance** - Students don't know which companies they're eligible for or what skills they need
2. **Resume Quality Issues** - Poor resumes that don't pass ATS screening
3. **Skill Gaps** - Students unaware of missing skills required by target companies
4. **Interview Anxiety** - Insufficient practice and feedback for interviews
5. **No Centralized Platform** - Fragmented tools for resume, preparation, and tracking
6. **Placement Officers Lack Insights** - No data-driven view of student readiness

There is a need for a comprehensive AI-powered platform that provides personalized placement preparation, company matching, skill analysis, and interview practice.
