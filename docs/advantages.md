# Advantages

1. **AI-Powered**: Leverages Google Gemini AI and ML for intelligent analysis
2. **Personalized**: Tailored recommendations based on individual student profiles
3. **Comprehensive**: Covers entire placement journey from resume to interviews
4. **Cost-Effective**: No paid third-party APIs for video/audio (uses browser APIs)
5. **Privacy-First**: Face analysis runs entirely on-device, no data leaves browser
6. **Scalable**: Microservices architecture, can handle thousands of students
7. **Modern UI**: Professional glassmorphism design with dark/light modes
8. **Data-Driven**: Analytics and insights for informed decision making
9. **Role-Based**: Separate dashboards for students, officers, and admins
10. **Production Ready**: Deployable to Vercel, Render, and cloud platforms
